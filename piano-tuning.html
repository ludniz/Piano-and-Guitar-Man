PIANO TUNING & REPAIRS

Pianos should be tuned at least once a year in order for the piano to work at optimum performance. Neglecting of tuning and servicing of the instrument regularly results in poor performance and always results in costly repairs. Caring for the piano and making sure of the correct location of the piano in the house is of vital importance.

Repairs and tuning of pianos include: Performing repairs on the action, hammers and the replacement of different parts and felts on the piano like damper felt, installing of new tuning pins, repair and install new strings. Sometimes I have to make new parts for the action to work properly.

I tune and repair pianos anywhere in South Africa where my services are needed.
